General Health Tips

Everyday practices to stay healthy regardless of the weather

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily. Increase intake during hot or humid weather to prevent dehydration.

Sun Protection

Apply SPF 30+ sunscreen before going outside, especially between 10am–4pm when UV rays are strongest.

Breathe Better

On high pollution days, limit outdoor activities. Use an air purifier indoors and keep windows closed.

Dress Smart

Layer your clothing in cold weather. Light, breathable fabrics are best in heat to regulate body temperature.

Monitor Your Health

People with asthma, heart disease, or diabetes should pay extra attention to weather changes that can trigger symptoms.

Rest Well

Maintain 7–9 hours of sleep. Extreme temperatures can disrupt sleep — keep your bedroom cool and comfortable.

About This Application

WeatherHealth is a final year Computer Science project developed to address the growing need for weather-aware health information. The system integrates real-time meteorological data with user health profiles to generate targeted health advisories.

The application retrieves live weather parameters including temperature, humidity, UV index, wind speed, and air quality index through the OpenWeatherMap API, and applies a rule-based advisory engine to produce relevant health recommendations for each user.
